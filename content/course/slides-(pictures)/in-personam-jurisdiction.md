# Bases for in Personam Jurisdiction

<!-- page: 1 -->

> **Diagram type:** Structured outline comparing general and specific in personam jurisdiction.

## Text Extracted from Document

**General:** No relationship between forum and subject matter of lawsuit needed.

- Really at home
  - State(s) of defendant citizenship: for corp. and humans, use diversity rules
  - Does not require long-arm statute, ***OR***
- *Daimler*, "At home": need
  1. Long-arm statute, **plus/and**
  2. Substantial, continuous, and systematic contacts: for business, location of place where large fraction (>50%?) of business, ***OR***
- Consent, either
  - Failure to object (quickly!), ***OR***
  - Advanced consent: forum selection clause
- Tag? (not sure, see *Burnham*)

**Specific:** Requires

1. Long-arm statute, **plus/and**
2. Permissible under the 14th Amendment's due process clause, requires
   - a. Minimal contacts linking defendant, subject matter of litigation, forum, **plus/and**
   - b. Fair and reasonable, consider:
     - Extent of defendant's contacts
     - Defendant convenience
     - Plaintiff's interest/convenience
     - Forum's interest
     - Interest of judicial system, including location of witnesses, evidence
     - Choice of law
