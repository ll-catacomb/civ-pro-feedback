# *Chauffeurs, Teamsters & Helpers Local No. 391 v. Terry*

<!-- page: 1 -->

> **Diagram type:** Two-part diagram drawing an analogy between a trust structure (top) and a union structure (bottom). The red arrows show the parallel relationship: Trust ↔ Another business maps to Union ↔ Employer (via CBA). The analogy illustrates that a union's duty of fair representation to its members resembles a trustee's fiduciary duty — which would be equitable. However, because the remedy sought was backpay (compensatory damages — a legal remedy), the Supreme Court held the plaintiffs were entitled to a jury trial under the 7th Amendment.
