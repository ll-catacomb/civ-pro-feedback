## Description

The diagram illustrates two ways a wealthy person can transfer assets:

**Trust (left side):**
- The rich person funds a **Trust** with money ($$$$)
- The Trust is managed by a **Trustee** under a **contract**
- During the grantor's lifetime, the Trust distributes to **life beneficiaries** ($$$)
- Upon death, the Trust distributes to **death beneficiaries** ($$$)
- The trust avoids probate and operates outside the estate

**Will/Estate (right side):**
- Upon death, assets pass into the **Estate**
- A **Will** directs the estate to distribute to **legatees** ($$$)
- This route goes through probate
