# Preclusion — Claim Preclusion and Issue Preclusion

<!-- page: 1 -->

> **Diagram type:** Two-column reference slide comparing elements of claim preclusion and issue preclusion.

## Text Extracted from Document

### Claim Preclusion

1) same claim, **and**
2) same party (or in privity), **and**
3) a judgment that is both
   - a. final, **and**
   - b. "on the merits"

*plus other stuff*
- rule in *Marresse*
- rendering court can limit claim preclusive effect of judgment

### Issue Preclusion

1) same issue, **and**
2) actually litigated, **and**
3) necessary to decision
4) ??? mutuality ???

*plus other stuff*
- judgment of sufficient quality/finality
- sufficient opportunity, incentive to litigate
- only preclude party who lost in L1, or those in privity
