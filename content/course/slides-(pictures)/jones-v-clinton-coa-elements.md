**Elements of the cause of action**

1. Refusal to engage in relationship
2. Such refusal caused . . .
3. Cognizable employment harm, such as:
   - Denial of promotion applications
   - Discouragement from applying for promotion
   - Lower wages/lost wage increases
   - Freezing out of the job
   - Substantial decrease in responsibilities (such that, *e.g.*, lower opportunities for advancement)
