# Summary Judgment Mechanics — *Celotex* Framework

<!-- page: 1 -->

> **Diagram type:** Decision tree / flowchart showing the burden-shifting analysis for motions for summary judgment under the *Celotex* framework.

## Text Extracted from Document

**Who bears burden of persuasion on claim at trial?**
(Assume burden of persuasion is preponderance of the evidence — more likely than not)

---

### If Non-Moving Party (NMP) bears burden of persuasion:

MP must make a showing (bears burden of production) that, if unrebutted, shows no genuine issue of material fact such that MP is entitled to judgment as a matter of law. MP can fulfill this burden in one of two ways:

1. Produce evidence that "knocks out" a required element of NMP's burden of persuasion — *i.e.*, evidence that, if unrebutted, would force a rational jury to conclude the element NMP must prove is in fact not true
2. Show lack of evidence in record sufficient for NMP to meet burden of persuasion, such that unless state of evidence changes, no rational jury could conclude NMP will meet trial burden of persuasion

**Did MP meet this burden of production?**
- No → MFSJ fails
- Yes → Burden of production shifts to NMP to provide evidence demonstrating an issue of material fact by:
  - producing new evidence, OR
  - arguing that what was in MP's submission actually is sufficient to create a genuine issue of material fact such that a rational jury could find in NMP's favor

**Did NMP meet this burden of production?**
- Yes → MFSJ fails
- No → MFSJ succeeds

---

### If Moving Party (MP) bears burden of persuasion:

MP must produce evidence (bears burden of persuasion) that, if unrebutted, shows no genuine issue of material fact such that a rational decisionmaker could only conclude that MP met its preponderance-of-evidence burden of persuasion, entitling MP to judgment as a matter of law; no other conclusion would be rational.

**Did MP meet this burden of production?**
- No → MFSJ fails
- Yes → Burden of production shifts to NMP to provide evidence there is a genuine issue of material fact and therefore case needs a trial
  - **Did NMP meet this burden of production?**
    - Yes → MFSJ fails
    - No → MFSJ succeeds
