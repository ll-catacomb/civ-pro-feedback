### Substantial Control

Example:
- Sole owner of a corporation ("close" corporation)
  - Owner induces corporation to contract
  - Other party allegedly breaches
  - Lawsuit 1: by corporation against other party, for damages
  - Lawsuit 2: by owner, for loss of value of shares
