# Greiner Civil Procedure Version 02


## Export Customization Tips

We designed this opencasebook.org export using MS Word Styles to simplify customization. The "Styles Pane" in Word's "Home" tab shows a list of styles in the document, and the active style of your current element or selection.
For example, you can quickly change the case text font for the whole document by modifying the "Case Body" style. The style modification screen also shows which style your style is "Based On." If you modify the style listed there, all styles based on it will also change. Some older documents might use different styles than newer ones— click into an element and check the Styles Pane to confirm its style.
Check out Microsoft's  for the finer points and caveats.
**Remember:** style modifications can yield sweeping results that aren't always apparent on the page you're viewing. Make a backup copy of your document before experimenting.
